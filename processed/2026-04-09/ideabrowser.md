## Techniques & Methods
- **Revenue-loop bootstrapping (Nike / Phil Knight method)** — Each batch of sales funds the next purchase order; keep your day job until three signals are all true: last batch covered next order with profit left over, you can name the month side income passes salary, the day job feels like the side project.
- **Parts-bin product development** — Failed projects are a code/feature library; build the next product from salvaged components (Testimonial.to built in 3 weeks reusing code from 4 failed projects).
- **Acquisition buyer as first customer** — For data businesses with privacy constraints, sell the premium acquisition report ($500+/report) before building subscriptions; validates willingness to pay without needing critical mass of benchmarking data.
- **Wedge → expand category model** — Start with one vertically deep category (laundromats), achieve density, then expand horizontally; eventually license the dataset to lenders and insurers.
