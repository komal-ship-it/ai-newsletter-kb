## Techniques & Methods
- **Sales Safari** (Amy Hoy) — 30-50 hours of lurking in forums where target customers complain, with structured notes across four quadrants: Pain, Jargon, Worldview, Recommendations. Produces marketing copy that converts because it's pulled verbatim from customer language.
- **The Pain Ladder** — Four-stage framework for qualifying opportunity depth:
  1. Curiosity ("Is there a way to...") → content
  2. Frustration ("Why can't this just...") → feature
  3. Resignation ("I've given up on...") → whole product
  4. Workaround ("I just use a spreadsheet for...") → competitor
- **Examples as Product** — "Automated email response examples" searched after paying for the tool. Any product with an empty text box creates a second market for templates that make it usable.
