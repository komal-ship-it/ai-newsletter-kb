## Techniques & Methods
- **Compliance as a moat** — In regulated markets, you compete on permission, not features. Someone can clone your app in a weekend — they cannot clone your compliance paperwork without a lawyer. The regulatory sign-off process that slows your launch is the same process a competitor must replicate.
- **Regulated vertical GTM** — Start with one well-documented vertical (HIPAA for healthcare). Use 10-15 beta clients to reveal where templates break. The analytics from pilots become the purchasing case for administrators.
- **Zappos "The Offer" / Quit-Offer Audit** — Offer new hires one week's pay to quit at end of week one. Track how many take the money and how many of those who stayed leave within 90 days. The gap reveals the quality of your hiring filter.
- **Conviction as the hiring filter** — Skill is table stakes. Hsieh rejected skilled candidates who didn't fit culture. Result: reps who made 10-hour customer calls and sent flowers without permission — no script required.
