## Key Announcements
- **"AI Is the Next Printing Press" essay** — Sophie Levy argues AI's impact depends on whether humans develop interpersonal skills first, drawing on the printing press as a historical analogue. Core thesis: AI used to *avoid* discomfort erodes trust; AI used to *scale* skills learned in person strengthens it.

## Tools & Products
- **The Magnet Collective** — Organizational program combining in-person early-career leadership development with AI-based skill reinforcement | https://www.themagnetcollective.net/ | Effort: major (org adoption)
- **Tuffy** — AI tool that reinforces reflective practice and scales skill development over time | https://www.tough.day/ | Effort: quick-win to evaluate

## Techniques & Methods
- **Two-stage human-AI learning model** — (1) Develop human skills locally, relationally, and in person. (2) Reinforce and scale those skills through AI tools. AI extends what was built in person; it does not substitute for building it.
- **Credibility-through-responsibility model** — Assign early-career employees real, consequential projects with tenured colleagues as audience. AI tools then extend the credibility earned through those experiences.

## Research Papers
- **WEF New Economy Skills: Unlocking the Human Advantage (2025)** — Employers prioritize analytical thinking, creativity, resilience, and leadership. Notes uncertainty about which skills genAI will meaningfully improve. Supports cautious approach: assume some interpersonal skills will not be automated. | https://reports.weforum.org/docs/WEF_New_Economy_Skills_Unlocking_the_Human_Advantage_2025.pdf

## Industry Trends
- AI adoption is happening on organizational timescales, compressing the governance timeline that took decades with past technology transitions (printing press took centuries to stabilize)
- WEF identifies "AI-irreplaceable" skills: self-understanding, awareness, creativity, resilience
- Loneliness, burnout, disconnection costs: Cigna estimates significant financial impact on workplace productivity
- "Trust isn't restored through intention — it's earned through participation" (Chappy Asel, AI Collective)
