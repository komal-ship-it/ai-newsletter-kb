## Techniques & Methods
- **The Third-Place Test** — Ask: "If a competitor built your top feature at half the price, would customers switch?" If yes, you're selling a transaction. The unclonable thing is your environment. (Starbucks case study: couches outlasted hundreds of drink changes.)
- **VA Marketplace Moat via Slow Vetting** — Manually screening 30-50 VAs before launch creates a defensible moat; the operational slowness is the product differentiation.
