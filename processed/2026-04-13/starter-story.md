## Techniques & Methods
- **Prove the Market Before Building** — Reverse-engineer what's already working: find a proven market, validate revenue benchmarks, then build. Contrast with the common mistake of building on instinct alone.
- **Hyper-Niche Positioning** — "Calorie tracker for wrestling athletes trying to make weight" beats generic "calorie tracker." Speak directly to one specific person until they say "that's exactly what I'm looking for." Applied by multiple case studies this issue.
- **Single Partnership Growth Lever** — German app dev reached $30K/month with one strategic partnership rather than paid ads or viral content. Finding one distribution amplifier beats running many acquisition channels weakly.
