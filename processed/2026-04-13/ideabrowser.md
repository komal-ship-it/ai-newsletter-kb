## Techniques & Methods
- **Believability-Weighted Decision Making** — Ray Dalio / Bridgewater method: weight votes by each person's track record on that specific type of decision (score 1-3), not seniority or volume. Score 3 = strong track record, 1 = no relevant experience. Multiply input by score. Requires transparency: record meetings, publish feedback, make performance ratings visible.
- **Micro-SaaS Acquisition Financial Checklist** — Build the financial tier first (revenue verification + churn cohorts); it's repeatable and cheap. Codebase review is the bottleneck — requires a developer who knows the stack. First reports go at cost to buyers with 30-day closing deadlines.
- **White-Label B2B Distribution** — White-label your audit product to brokers (Acquire.com, Flippa) who pay because vetted deals close faster. Reduces direct CAC.
