## Techniques & Methods
- **The Default State thesis (Oana Ruxandra)** — AI-enabled creation is a return to pre-industrial versatility, not disruption. 80-90% of people worked across many skills before industrialization. The 40-year single-role career is ~150 years old and ending. Implication: build for generalists re-entering the economy, not specialists defending niches.
- **Music industry as early-mover analogy** — Gatekeepers (Warner, etc.) protected distribution, not quality. When barriers fell, previously excluded creators flooded in. Software/AI is one industry cycle behind music (2015 → now). Same dynamics apply to who builds products and why.
- **Founder signal data** — 1-in-3 US adults planning a business in 2026 (+94% YoY). 65% plan to use AI. LinkedIn "founder" profiles +60% YoY, nearly tripled since 2022. Small business applications above 5M/year for 5 consecutive years.
