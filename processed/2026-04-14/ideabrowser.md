## Techniques & Methods
- **GitHub as sales intelligence** — Monitor target company GitHub accounts for: framework migrations (= rewrite in progress), new infra repos (= budget moving), contributor growth spikes (= team expansion). Stronger signal than paid intent data vendors; public and free.
- **Pre-launch distribution spec (James Clear model)** — Pick one topic; one post/week; same audience; same channel; goal is email subscribers not followers; run for 6–12 months before building. Launch signal: subscribers start asking "when are you selling something?"
