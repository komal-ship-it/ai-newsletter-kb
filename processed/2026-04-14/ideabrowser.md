## Techniques & Methods
- **Threshold Map (Walmart Pattern)** — Map the largest player's floor (minimum deal size, customer size, geography). Count everything below that line — that's the beachhead. Walton targeted towns Kmart wouldn't enter; $55B by 1992.
- **CRM as Institutional Memory** — CRM tools for customer service trending +979% because routing logic and cross-channel history become embedded institutional knowledge. The CRM becomes the hire that never leaves.
- **ABZ Framework** — Plan Z first (boring achievable fallback, one sentence). Plan A is current bet. Plan B can't be written yet — reveals itself after Plan A teaches you something. Source: Reid Hoffman / LinkedIn origin story.
