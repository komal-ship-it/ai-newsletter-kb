## Techniques & Methods
- **Human-first, AI-amplified framework** — Build human skills (communication, collaboration, decision-making) in person and relationally first; then use AI tools to extend, scale, and reinforce those skills. AI used as substitute for discomfort weakens social trust; used as rehearsal, it strengthens performance.
- **Capstone project model for early-career development** — Early-career professionals lead a real project for tenured colleagues to build credibility through responsibility, not just training.
