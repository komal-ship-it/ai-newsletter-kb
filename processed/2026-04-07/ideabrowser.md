## Techniques & Methods
- **Basecamp "appetite" circuit breaker** — Before building, set a fixed time budget based on what the feature is worth (not how long it'll take). Scope bends to meet the deadline, not vice versa. 6-week cycles, 2-week cooldowns. Zombie project test: if you can't assign a week-budget to a project, it has no appetite — kill it or define one.
- **Zombie project test** — For each project: "I would spend [X weeks] on this, and not a day more." No number = no appetite = kill it or reframe.
