## Techniques & Methods
- **AI as Rehearsal, Not Substitute** — AI becomes corrosive when used to avoid interpersonal difficulty (where trust and discernment are built), and constructive when it reinforces capacities developed in-person first. Framework: develop human skills locally/relationally → reinforce via AI.
- **Two-Stage Adoption Model** — (1) Humans must develop human skills in person; (2) AI tools then extend those capacities at scale. Skipping step 1 weakens social trust and productivity.
