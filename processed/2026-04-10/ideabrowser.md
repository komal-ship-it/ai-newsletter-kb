## Techniques & Methods
- **Seth Godin's Smallest Viable Audience** — Resist broadening: find the tightest group you can serve and stay there. One person who feels seen tells three. "Who's it for?" must be answerable in one sentence. If the answer shifts quarter to quarter, you're chasing the market instead of owning a corner of it.
- **The 10-Name Test** — Write down the first AND last names of 10 people who would miss your product if it disappeared tomorrow. If you're describing types instead of naming people, you've found a demographic (requires ad spend), not an audience (sells itself).
- **Classmold GTM Strategy** — Partner with 10 teachers across subjects in districts where interactive learning standards are in evaluation frameworks. Use real-unit pilots to generate analytics that become the purchasing case for administrators.
