## AI Copilot for Managers

The Problem: There are 83 million managers in the United States alone. Most are winging it. The typical first-time manager gets promoted because they were great at their individual job. The result? Disengaged teams, missed reviews, vague feedback. The global cost of poor management is estimated at over $8.8 trillion in lost productivity every year.

The Solution:

Idea: An AI-powered copilot for people managers

Product:
- Connect calendar → AI joins every 1:1, captures conversation, logs action items, sends pre-meeting brief
- After each meeting: AI analyses patterns — "you've spoken 80% of the time in your last 4 meetings with Sarah" or "you haven't given James positive feedback in 6 weeks"
- At review season: auto-drafts full performance reviews from year's worth of 1:1 transcripts, sentiment data, and logged feedback

Business Model: $25-50/month per manager self-serve; $10-30k/year enterprise deals through HR teams

End Goal: Get acquired by Workday, BambooHR, Microsoft (Teams), Lattice, or Culture Amp at 8-12x ARR
