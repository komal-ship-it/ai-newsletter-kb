### IDEA: AI Copilot for Managers

"A Few Good Managers"

The Problem: There are 83 million managers in the United States alone. Most of them are winging it. The typical first-time manager gets promoted because they were great at their individual job - which has absolutely nothing to do with running a team. The global cost of poor management is estimated at over $8.8 trillion in lost productivity every year.

The Solution:
- The Idea: An AI-powered copilot for people managers to help them to become great managers.
- Product: Connect your calendar and the AI automatically joins every 1:1, captures the conversation, logs action items, and sends you a pre-meeting brief before each session - surfacing what was discussed last time, open items, and suggested talking points based on how each direct report is trending. After each meeting, the AI analyses patterns across your entire team - flagging things like "you've spoken 80% of the time in your last 4 meetings with Sarah" or "you haven't given James positive feedback in 6 weeks." At review season, the copilot auto-drafts a full performance review for each team member from the year's worth of 1:1 transcripts.
- Business Model: $25-50/month per manager self-serve; $10-30k/year enterprise deals sold through HR teams.
- End Goal: Get acquired by Workday, BambooHR, Microsoft (Teams), or Lattice or Culture Amp. Target exit 8-12x ARR.

### How to Use Google Stitch Like a Pro

Google Stitch just became one of the most powerful free tools available to founders - it turns plain English into high-fidelity UI designs in minutes. Steps:
1. Go to stitch.withgoogle.com and sign in with your Google account.
2. Before you prompt, define two things: who is using this, and how do you want them to feel?
3. Hit the '+' button and use the prompt enhancer. It translates vague words like "modern" or "sporty" into specific design language.
4. Tell Stitch your colour hierarchy explicitly: Neutral for the canvas, Primary for key UI elements, Tertiary for your loudest accents.
