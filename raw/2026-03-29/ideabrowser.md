## IDEA OF THE DAY
**Client retention agent for freelance fitness trainers** — Liftoff
March 29, 2026

Independent trainers treat every lost client as a sales problem. Find the next one, fill the slot, keep moving. Replacing a client costs three times what keeping them would have. The signs showed up weeks before the cancellation text. The trainer's booking software saw all of it and said nothing.

Liftoff is an AI retention agent that reads client behavior between sessions and catches drift before it becomes dropout. A client who trained three times a week for months drops to twice. Then once. Then cancels. Liftoff sees the pattern at the first missed session, not the last. When engagement fades, it fires a re-engagement: a check-in timed to the shift, a milestone callout, a session adjustment.

To build it, connect to Mindbody or Trainerize through existing APIs and build a risk score for each client based on attendance and engagement history. Before writing a single alert, run the prediction model against historical dropout data from 10 active trainers.

Individual trainers pay $75/month. Corporate wellness programs pay $2,000/year. One retained client covers three months of subscription in a single invoice.
