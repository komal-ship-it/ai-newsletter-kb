## Bloomberg Terminal for Science Breakthroughs

*Lab-solutely essential*

Demo: https://scifind-terminal-286359934050.us-west1.run.app

**The Problem:** Inspired by The Infinity Machine (biography of Demis Hassabis). AI is compressing decades of drug discovery and materials science into years, and the 4 million papers published annually is about to go vertical. How are companies going to keep up?

**The Idea:** A Bloomberg-style scientific intelligence platform that monitors every major journal globally and surfaces cross-field breakthroughs for organisations that compete on scientific knowledge.

**Product:**
* A pharma company, hedge fund, or consulting firm connects their focus areas and the platform maps the entire global research landscape against their strategic priorities 24/7.
* Every morning, decision-makers get a tailored intelligence briefing — their sector's top developments plus a "Cross-Field Spark" flagging breakthroughs from adjacent disciplines before anyone else catches on.
* Deep-dive mode lets teams query the entire scientific literature like a conversation — surfacing sources, contradictions, and open questions automatically. A VP of Strategy at a pharma company can get the answer to "what does the latest GLP-1 research mean for our pipeline?" in minutes.

**Prototyping:**
* Demo: https://scifind-terminal-286359934050.us-west1.run.app
* Demo prompt: https://www.notion.so/Demo-Prompt-SciFind-33d8d2b9402b806c9df0f0725020f44a

**Business Model:** $50k–$500k/year enterprise contracts with pharma companies, hedge funds, and consulting firms.

**End Goal:** Acquisition by Bloomberg, Elsevier, Springer Nature, or major pharma (Pfizer, AstraZeneca). Target exit $500M–$2B at 8–12x ARR.

## How to Build Your First Agent with Claude

Claude released "Managed Agents" — surprisingly easy to spin up a first AI agent, no deep technical knowledge required.

**Step-by-step:**
1. Head to Claude Console (https://console.anthropic.com/) → Quickstart under Managed Agents.
2. Define what you want your agent to do, or pick one of the pre-built templates. The clearer the job description, the better your agent performs.
3. Work with Claude inside the Console to refine behaviour, test against real inputs, and deploy when ready.
