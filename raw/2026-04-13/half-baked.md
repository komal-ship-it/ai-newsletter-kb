## 🏊 Lead Gen for Pool Companies — "Making a Splash"

The Problem: There are over 10 million residential swimming pools in the USA with 500K-1M new pools built each year. Pool builders are still finding customers the old-fashioned way — buying shared leads from HomeAdvisor, running Facebook ads.

The Idea: A lead generation system that uses AI to identify houses with empty backyards and renders a pool in their backyard.

Product:
- System scans satellite imagery for mid-market homes ($500K-$1.2M) without pools, filtering by lot size, sun exposure, recent ownership changes
- AI renders a luxury pool into their actual backyard, calculates build cost and home value lift
- Generates cinematic video walkthrough; personalized postcard with before/after and QR code mailed automatically

Business Model: $50-100 per qualified lead, or $3-5k/mo premium tier. End Goal: Acquired by Angi, HomeAdvisor, ServiceTitan, or Jobber.

## 📧 How to Write Cold Emails That Actually Get Replies

1. Kill the subject line first — write something specific enough that only one person could have received it
2. Keep the whole email to five lines — hook, why it matters to them, your ask, a low-friction CTA, sign off. "Got 5 min for a quick yes/no?" is something they can actually answer
3. Follow up without apology — most replies come from follow-ups not the first email. Day 3: bump. Day 7: "worth a look or should I close this out?"
4. Track and iterate — send 100 emails, track what gets replies, kill what doesn't
