## IDEA OF THE DAY
**Offline field reporting tool for park rangers and wildlife agencies** — Fieldpin
March 31, 2026

Rangers carry smartphones into the field and fill out paper forms. The software their agencies run needs connectivity. The places they work have none. Field data degrades between observation and entry. Conservation decisions run on whatever survives the trip back to the station. Millions in conservation funding depend on data collected with a pencil.

Fieldpin is a mobile field reporting tool built for rangers who work where connectivity doesn't reach. Drop a pin for a wildlife sighting, log an incident, flag a trail condition. Everything saves locally and syncs when signal returns. The forms match the reports agencies file, so nothing gets re-entered at a desk. Each pin feeds a cross-agency dataset that builds over time: migration corridors, population trends, habitat encroachment patterns that no single park could map alone.

The MVP is an offline-first mobile app with pre-loaded form templates for common ranger tasks. Pilot with 10 rangers across 2 parks before building any dashboard features.

Charge $10 to $50 per user per month, with annual agency contracts for full deployments.
