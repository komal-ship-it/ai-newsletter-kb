STARTUP IDEA: AI NUTRITIONIST FOR ENDURANCE ATHLETES

The Problem: Everyone's an athlete these days — run clubs, half marathons, Hyroxes. These athletes spend hundreds on a Garmin, expensive running shoes, and energy gels, but then fuel themselves with whatever they fancy. A proper dietitian runs $200+ per session and still won't have access to your actual training data. For 50 million recreational runners, cyclists, and triathletes, that's a very expensive guess.

The Idea: An AI nutritionist that connects to your fitness wearable and generates daily fuelling plans for athletes.

Product:
- Connect your wearable on day one — the AI pulls your training history, calculates your baseline calorie needs, and builds a personalised nutrition profile based on your sport, goals, and body metrics.
- You add in your training schedule and get a daily fuel plan that matches what you're actually doing — high-carb pre-load before intervals, protein-heavy recovery on rest days, carb periodisation in race week. Adjusts in real time as your training load shifts.
- Specific meal suggestions, supplement timing (creatine, caffeine, electrolytes), and race-day nutrition windows all tied to your actual schedule.

Business Model: $19/month subscription. B2B2C through running clubs, triathlon teams, and coaching platforms. Whitelabel version for coaches at scale.

End Goal: Acquired by a wearable company (Garmin, Polar, Wahoo) or sports nutrition brand (Precision Hydration, Maurten) wanting a direct-to-athlete data layer. Target exit 8-12x ARR.
