### IDEA: AI Nutritionist for Endurance Athletes

"Fork to finish"

The Problem: 50 million recreational runners, cyclists, and triathletes need a sports nutritionist, but proper advice is either too generic or too expensive. A proper dietitian runs $200+ per session and won't have access to your actual training data.

The Solution:
- The Idea: An AI nutritionist that connects to your fitness wearable and generates daily fuelling plans for athletes.
- Product: Connect your wearable on day one - the AI pulls your training history, calculates your baseline calorie needs, and builds a personalised nutrition profile. You get a daily fuel plan that matches what you're actually doing - high-carb pre-load before intervals, protein-heavy recovery on rest days, carb periodisation in race week. Specific meal suggestions, supplement timing, and race-day nutrition windows all tied to your actual schedule.
- Business Model: $19/month subscription. B2B2C through running clubs, triathlon teams, and coaching platforms. Whitelabel version for coaches at scale.
- End Goal: Acquired by a wearable company (Garmin, Polar, Wahoo) or sports nutrition brand (Precision Hydration, Maurten). Target exit 8-12x ARR.
