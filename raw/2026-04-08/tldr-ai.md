S3 FILES AND THE CHANGING FACE OF S3
S3 Files lets developers mount any S3 bucket or prefix as a filesystem on an EC2 instance, container, or Lambda function. It is backed by EFS. From an application's perspective, S3 Files is a mounted directory, but from S3's perspective, the data is objects in a bucket.
