IDEA OF THE DAY: Multi-channel dashboard for YouTube portfolio operators ("Conduit")

Problem: YouTube Studio handles one channel at a time. Uploading the same video across five channels means 5 logins, 5 titles, 5 thumbnails, 5 scheduling screens. Creators who figured out how to scale production are stuck scaling admin work.

Solution: Conduit — multiple YouTube accounts in a single interface.
- One dashboard, every channel
- Upload a video once, deploy across channels with per-channel titles, thumbnails, descriptions
- Schedule across accounts without switching logins
- Portfolio-wide analytics on one screen
- AI layer trained on each channel's history: generates thumbnail variations, writes comment responses in each channel's voice

First build: bulk upload + cross-channel scheduling via YouTube API; drag-and-drop interface; per-channel metadata templates. Ship to 3 operators running 5+ channels.

Technical constraint: YouTube API rate limit — 10 simultaneous channel connections need batching logic.

Pricing:
- $97/month: 3-5 channels
- $199/month: 6-10 channels
- $299/month: unlimited

Distribution: faceless YouTube communities, creator Discord servers.
