## 🧠 Historical Figure Mentorship Platform — "Dead Useful"

The Problem: Meta is [building an AI clone of Mark Zuckerberg](https://www.fastcompany.com/91526258/meta-is-reportedly-building-a-mark-zuckerberg-ai-clone) so employees can get direct feedback and mentorship. Why not unlock some of history's greatest minds for all of us?

The Idea: An AI mentorship platform where users get coached 1-on-1 by historically accurate AI versions of the world's greatest minds (Jobs, Buffett, Aurelius, Feynman).

Product:
- Pick your mentor and complete a 10-minute onboarding capturing goals, challenges, business stage, and personality type
- Every session is a live conversation — you bring a real problem and your mentor responds in character
- Platform tracks progress, holds you accountable, surfaces relevant moments from the genius's life

[Try the demo](https://posthumentor-344780963302.us-west1.run.app) | [Get the demo prompt](https://loud-particle-7d0.notion.site/Demo-Prompt-Posthumentor-3428d2b9402b80f5bd60cfcd5d9723da)

Business Model: $99/month for one mentor, $149/month unlimited. End Goal: Acquired by Masterclass.
