Half Baked Fully Baked #13 — Sunday deep-dive edition.

IDEA: HelloFresh for Meal Prep
~70% of meal kit subscribers churn within 12 months. By Wednesday you're dreading the same chicken and rice.

Product: Weekly subscription shipping pre-portioned bulk base ingredients + modular flavour kits — cook once Sunday, get 5 distinctly different meals. App tracks kits used, suggests combos, logs macros to fitness tracker.
https://mealprept-286359934050.us-west1.run.app/

VALIDATION:
- 3 founder calls: 'If you can't raise $10M+ before you launch, don't bother.' Cold-chain logistics break at state lines.
- Pre-order test: $420 Meta ad spend, $1 deposit page → 4.2% conversion rate. 2 of 7 asked about flexibility before signing up (churn signal disguised as feature request).

BUSINESS MODEL:
- Single $79/week, Couples $129/week, Family $179/week.
- ~25% contribution margin after COGS, cold-chain shipping, fulfilment.
- Blue Apron's CAC jumped from $94 to $460; retention at 11 months: 15% Blue Apron, 9% HelloFresh, 5% Marley Spoon.

COMPETITIVE LANDSCAPE:
- HelloFresh: 3.7M active US customers, stuck in 'cook every night' paradigm.
- Factor/Freshly: heat-and-eat, kills the meal prep ritual.
- Trifecta: closest competitor for athletes, limited flavour modularity.

VERDICT: NO-GO. Product idea is strong but requires $10M+ to launch in a few cities, tight margins, and HelloFresh can match the wedge as a feature the moment you prove it.
