AI PODCAST RECORDING COMPANION

The Problem: Jason Calacanis wants to pay someone $5,000 to build the AI version of a podcast production crew. Howard Stern had Gary, Robin, Fred and Jackie - a full production crew that turned a radio show into a $500M empire. This is the idea.

The Solution:
- The Idea: A real-time AI crew that listens to your podcast as you record and feeds you live fact-checks, context, jokes and news updates, like having a production crew in your ear.
- Product: Creators plug in via browser or desktop app and the tool listens to the live audio stream in real time. Four AI personas (Fact-checker, Context Provider, Comedy Writer, News Anchor) activates automatically when relevant. The Fact-checker flags dodgy statistics mid-sentence. The Context Provider drops background color. The Comedy Writer generates one-liners tied to the current topic. The News Anchor surfaces relevant breaking stories.
- Business Model: $49/mo for indie creators, $199/mo for teams with multi-guest support. Enterprise licensing for podcast networks and live event producers.
- End Goal: Acquired by Spotify, iHeart, or a major podcast hosting platform looking to own the creator production layer. Target exit 8-12x ARR.

HOW TO USE SKILLS IN GOOGLE CHROME

Google just launched Skills in Gemini for Chrome - so instead of retyping the same prompt every time you hit a new page, you save it once and run it anywhere with a single click.

Step-by-step:
1. Click the Gemini button in the Chrome toolbar and write a prompt you know you'll reuse. When it works, save it as a Skill straight from your chat history.
2. Next time you need to use this Skill, type (/) or hit the (+) button to pull up your saved Skills, select one, pick which tabs to run it on, and fire.
3. Browse Google's built-in Skills library - macro calculators, product comparators, document scanners - install what you need and edit the prompt to fit.
