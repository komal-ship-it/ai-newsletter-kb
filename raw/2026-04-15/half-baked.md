## 🎙️ AI Podcast Recording Companion — "Stern Stuff"

The Problem: Jason Calacanis [wants to pay someone $5,000](https://x.com/twistartups/status/2044113723904712738) to build an AI production crew for podcasters. Howard Stern had Gary, Robin, Fred and Jackie — a full production crew that turned a radio show into a $500M empire.

The Idea: A real-time AI crew that listens to your podcast as you record and feeds you live fact-checks, context, jokes and news updates.

Product:
- Creators plug in via browser or desktop app, tool listens to live audio stream in real time
- Four AI personas: Fact-checker, Context Provider, Comedy Writer, News Anchor
- Output: clean version for regular listeners + "enhanced" version with AI sidebar for paid subscribers

[Try the demo](https://my-google-ai-studio-applet-344780963302.us-west1.run.app/) | [Get the demo prompt](https://www.notion.so/Demo-Prompt-Cuecrew-3438d2b9402b81d39908dfcd4f5fdcb2)

Business Model: $49/mo for indie creators, $199/mo for teams. End Goal: Acquired by Spotify, iHeart, or major podcast hosting platform.

## 🌐 How to Use Skills in Google Chrome

Google just launched [Skills in Gemini for Chrome](https://blog.google/products-and-platforms/products/chrome/skills-in-chrome) — save a prompt once and run it anywhere with a single click.

Step-by-step:
1. Click Gemini button in Chrome toolbar, write a reusable prompt, save it as a Skill
2. Type (/) or hit (+) to pull up saved Skills, select one, pick tabs to run it on
3. Browse Google's built-in Skills library for macro calculators, product comparators, document scanners

[Give it a try](https://chrome.google.com/gemini)
