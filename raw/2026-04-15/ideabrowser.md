## 📌 IDEA OF THE DAY: Promote-from-within engine for multi-unit franchise operators
April 15, 2026

Franchise operators blame turnover on pay or generational attitudes. The pattern is narrower: capable employees leave when they see no upward path. Corporate systems spend millions on leadership pipelines. A ten-location operator has no access to those programs.

**Ladderstep** replaces the informal tap-on-the-shoulder with a structured development track that runs across locations:
- Managers work through weekly 15-minute leadership scenarios by SMS
- Complete skill assessments tied to store metrics from 7shifts or Toast
- A peer circle connects them across brands
- Dashboard shows ownership who is advancing, stalling, or ready for multi-unit responsibility

Pricing: $300-$500 per location monthly. At 1,000 locations, annual revenue crosses $5M.

## 🔍 HIDDEN NICHE OPPORTUNITY: Freelance Debt Recovery Specialists

Solo debt collectors buy unpaid credit card balances from banks at 4-7 cents on the dollar. To take a debtor to court, they must prove chain of custody — a paper trail from the bank through each buyer. One missing document and the judge throws it out.

Opportunity: A paper trail that holds up in court. Collectors working 50 accounts piece this together by hand.

## 📚 FOUNDER PLAYBOOK: Two words of fallback, sixteen years of LinkedIn

Before starting LinkedIn in 2002, Reid Hoffman wrote down his Plan Z: go back to consulting. Two words. That clarity let him bet aggressively on a professional network.

**The ABZ Framework:**
- Plan Z first: Your boring, achievable fallback. If it took more than 10 seconds to write, simplify it.
- Plan A: Your current bet.
- Plan B: You can't write this yet — it reveals itself after Plan A teaches you something.

Z first removes fear from the math. A gives direction. B reveals itself.
