## IDEA OF THE DAY
**Conversation practice tool for managers who dread firing people** — Talktrack
April 2, 2026

Difficult conversations that don't happen don't disappear. Feedback that never lands becomes a surprise performance review. A performance issue pushed to next quarter becomes a termination that blindsides the team. Managers dodge these moments because avoidance feels safer than saying the wrong thing. The conversation happens anyway. It costs more every week it waits.

Talktrack is an AI simulation platform where managers practice the conversations they keep postponing. A scenario library covers performance reviews, conflict mediation, difficult feedback, and terminations. The AI role-player responds with real emotional reactions: defensiveness, deflection, silence, tears. Progress tracking identifies weak spots across sessions and measures improvement over time. A manager who has rehearsed a termination conversation twelve times walks into the real one with a different nervous system.

Five core scenarios is enough to start. Resist adding more until the role-player's emotional calibration makes managers feel genuine discomfort during practice. That discomfort is the product.

The institutional buyer is an HR department onboarding a new manager cohort. Individual pricing runs $79 to $199/month. Team packages run $500 to $2,000/year.

**The formula that tells you what to stop doing**

Your buyback rate exposes the hours you're wasting. Annual income / 2,000 / 4. A founder making $200K lands at $25/hour. Any task you can outsource for less than that number is a task you shouldn't be doing. Dan Martell built this formula after three exits (Spheric Technologies, Flowtown, Clarity.fm).
