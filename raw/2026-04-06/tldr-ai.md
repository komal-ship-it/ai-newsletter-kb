## SCIENCE & FUTURISTIC TECHNOLOGY

**IMPULSE SPACE, ANDURIL BUILDING SPACE TECHNOLOGY FOR GOLDEN DOME (3 MINUTE READ)**

Impulse Space and Anduril Industries have been selected to develop space-based missile interceptor technology for President Trump's planned Golden Dome missile defense shield. Space-based interceptors are a key but unproven component of the Golden Dome effort. The project is expected to cost $185 billion and demonstrate operational capability by 2028.

**SCIENTISTS BUILD LIVING ROBOTS WITH NERVOUS SYSTEMS (4 MINUTE READ)**

Neurobots are clusters of living cells that spontaneously self-organize in saline conditions to move and act in novel ways. Made with natural cells arranged manually by scientists. Scientists are still figuring out the organizing principles enabling these movements, with future uses including monitoring ecosystem health.
